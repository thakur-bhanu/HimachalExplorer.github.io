Kheerganga

Kullu Valley's must visit.

Located amidst the astounding Parvati Valley in the Kullu district of Himachal Pradesh, Kheerganga is considered the trekkerâ€™s paradise.Your trek begins from Kasol, which is a quaint village perched at a height of about 5000 ft. in the Himalayas. Located deep in the Parvati Valley, Kheerganga is considered the 'abode of Lord Shiva.' Known for mesmerizing hot water spring and scenic valley views, the Kheerganga Trek is located at a height of about 9600 ft. and is regarded as one of the most well-known treks to do in Himalaya.The peace and beauty can make you forget about the chaos of city life instantly. Kasol, the second destination of the trek is considered to be a favorite with the hippies and off-late has become popular with the Israelis -each one filled with interesting stories that will leave you mesmerized. While here, do not miss out on gorging at the plethora of Israeli cafes where you can unwind before leaving for the trek.

Kheerganga, known as the abode of Lord Shiva is nestled beautifully in Paravati Valley and enveloped in the mighty mountains that make it one of the most popular destinations to visit. It is a nature's paradise for the stunning vistas of snow-clad Himalayas and the glorious roar of the Parvati River will fascinate your inner being. One of the many reasons to embark on this adventurous trek is the natural hot water spring on the top known as Parvati Kund that will make your soul content with utmost bliss.
